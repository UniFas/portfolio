Who Am I?

Hey there! I'm Francis Santos a game developer and storyteller currently studying Game Design in Leiria, Portugal. My passion lies in crafting experiences that blend narrative depth, atmosphere, and a touch of the surreal.

My goal? To create interconnected worlds where games and stories echo each other where what you play, read, and feel all contribute to a larger universe. I focus on indie development, strong art direction, and experimental mechanics.
